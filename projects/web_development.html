Description:

This website was originally designed for a college assignment but I decided to actually build it, just for fun. This is my first ever hand coded website, as my previous website was a WordPress blog. The Website is designed to be a portfolio of my programming and other projects. It has a slider and lightbox plugins integrated into its design to help it meet this goal.

For another college assignment I had to make a contact form with validation. I completed this with PHP and I decided to integrate it into the contact page.

This was also the first time I used a Version Control System(GitHub) for a project. I am continuing to make additions to the website as my skill in web development increases.
